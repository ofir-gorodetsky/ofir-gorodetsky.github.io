Comments

  1. Here you can find a combinatorial question related to paper no. 4.
  2. Here you can find the SageMath code that generates the graphs presented in the paper no. 7. The function generate_all_graphs() generates the various graphs, but one first must run the MATLAB files 1 and 2 in the same folder.
  3. In subsection 3.4 of paper no. 14, it is mentioned that empirically, two symmetric Laurent polynomials given explicitly in that subsection, have certain constant term series (coinciding with the generating functions of sequences known as s18 and (γ)). This empirical observation has since been verified rigorously using the HolonomicFunctions Mathematica package.

  5. At this link you will find slides and recording of a talk, based mostly on papers no. 17 and no. 19 (on smooth integers and polynomials via the indirect saddle point method).
